Duration: 2 weeks up to maximum 12 weeks

Volunteers aged 18+

Duration Program Fee Average Expenses
2 weeks £1,866 134£/day

This is an in-country hands-on internship program, at the research center of the International Hellenic University in Thessaloniki (Greece) with provided shared housing and local team support.
Interns will have the opportunity to choose between four career paths: Drones, Electric Vehicles, Renewable Energy & Industry 5.0
Drones: Interns will have the opportunity to design, assemble, program drones and their sub-systems. The program is designed to be hands-on and allow the intern to work in various phases of the drone development including field work.
Electromobility: The participants will work across the spectrum of sustainable mobility solutions and sub-systems such as electric vehicles (cars, bikes, scooters), batteries, chargers, powertrains etc.

WindAid projects provide electricity to remote communities utilising wind energy to provide a clean and reliable source of energy in locations where traditional electricity is not available.
7 million Peruvians do not have access to electricity.
Access to electricity is a necessity for the quality of life of any human being living today. Many of us don't even think twice about turning on the light switch in our homes, but energy poverty severely limits the ability and opportunity for those without access to electricity to thrive. We want to change that.
